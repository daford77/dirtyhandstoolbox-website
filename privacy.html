Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

Dirty Hands Toolbox (“the App,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to privacy by design. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Dirty Hands Toolbox mobile app.

1) Offline-First by Design

Dirty Hands Toolbox is a 100% offline-first mobile application. The App is designed to function locally on your device without requiring an internet connection for core use.

Any content you create or manage in the App is stored using local device storage.

2) Data Collection and Transmission

Dirty Hands Toolbox does not collect, transmit, share, or sell personal data, telemetry, analytics, or vehicle information.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • No usage analytics or telemetry is collected.
  • No vehicle-related data is collected.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers.
  • No data is shared with third parties.
  • No data is sold.

3) Third-Party Services

Dirty Hands Toolbox does not rely on third-party analytics or advertising SDKs to profile users or track behavior.

4) Data Security

Because data is stored locally on your device, you control access through your device’s own security settings (such as passcode, biometric lock, and device encryption where available).

5) Children’s Privacy

Dirty Hands Toolbox does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children, because the App does not collect data.

6) Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7) Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@dirtyhandstoolbox.com.
